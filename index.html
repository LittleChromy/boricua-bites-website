About Us

At Boricua Bites, we're passionate about serving fast, delicious, high-quality Puerto Rican food at fair prices. We bring the authentic flavors and vibrant culture of Puerto Rico right to your table. Our team is dedicated to creating a welcoming environment where you can experience real Puerto Rican cuisine and hospitality.
